Daiki International Trading Corporation (DITCO)

Daiki International Trading Corporation (DITCO) is an international trading company focused on the buying and selling of aluminium ingots and scrap. DITCO also supports global market information gathering for raw material markets and related industries.

How we use SMS

DITCO uses SMS messaging only for business-related communication with our vendors, suppliers, and business partners. Typical messages include delivery coordination, scheduling, order status, logistics updates, and other operational questions related to aluminium and raw material trading.

Employees use a company communication platform to send and receive these messages as part of normal business operations.
